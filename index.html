Discovery and Prospection

Altough minning asteroids has long been considered science-fiction, it is a true possibility nowadays. Hence, it is imperative that we be able to determine suitable asteoids to mine. With more than 20,000 near-Earth objects (NEOs) larger than 100m diameter, minning asteroids can become a profitable industry.

Our solution incorporates remote sensing in order to discover minning potetntial of asteroids and, once the asteroids with potential are discovered, a satellite is sent to perform:

  • Spectroscopic characterization (Surface Mineral Content) λ=0,4-0,9[um]
  • Photometric (Rotation and Shape) λ=0,8-2,5[um] (Near IR band)
  • Thermal Infrared λ = 3,5-10 um
  • Radar and Interferometry
